WHO WE ARE

Hyundai Motor Company is a global automotive leader committed to creating innovative vehicles and mobility solutions that enrich people's lives. Our vision is simple yet powerful: Progress for Humanity. We don't just build cars—we create solutions for a better, more connected, and sustainable future.
